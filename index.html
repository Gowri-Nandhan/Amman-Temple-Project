About Our Temple

Chitharal Arulmigu Mutharamman Temple, also known as Mutharamman Kovil, is a sacred Hindu temple located in Chitharal, Tamil Nadu. Perched atop a picturesque hill, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Mutharamman, a powerful manifestation of Goddess Durga. The temple is renowned for its spiritual significance and its proximity to the ancient Chitharal Jain Monuments, which feature exquisite rock-cut sculptures and cave temples from the 9th century. Surrounded by lush greenery and offering breathtaking panoramic views, this temple is not just a place of worship but also a destination for history and nature enthusiasts.
